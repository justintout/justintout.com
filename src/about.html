about me

Hi. I'm a software engineer during the day (and a lot of times at night). I currently work in consumer IoT, designing systems and integrations for C by GE, GE Lighting's smart home brand. I've been called a full-stack engineer by my friends and I have a particular interest in cloud-native computing and Bluetooth Low Energy.

Like many, my interest in software started from an early fascination with the Internet. I loved trying to understand the systems and interactions in behind high-population online games. I first learned to code to create bots for RuneScape and NeoPets in the early 2000's. Puzzle Pirates taught me Excel, Poker, and economic theory. The game I have sunk the most time into, by far, is Path of Exile, because it's a goddamn masterpiece.

I have a few analog hobbies as well. I enjoy culinary arts in particular. My most recent focus has been fermented hot sauces. I also enjoy fishing, woodworking, and spending time with my family. My brother has recently gotten me interseted in CAD/CAM modeling. You can keep up with my (mostly culinary) endeavors on my Instagram.
